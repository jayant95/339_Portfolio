Hello! I am a recent graduate from Simon Fraser University with a B.Sc. in Interactive Systems. Previously, I worked at SAP as an intern where one of my many tasks included fixing front-end and back-end issues for SAP Analytics Cloud. Most recently, inspired by my interest in bodybuilding, I created Deltoyd, a web application tool for bodybuilders to uniquely compare their physiques with professional bodybuilders. Deltoyd also helps keep track of progression through muscle measurements, progress pictures and setting new goals to achieve their physique.

My technical background encompasses front-end and back-end technologies including JavaScript, Java, PHP, SQL, C#, React, JSON, XML, HTML and CSS. Within JavaScript I have also used D3.js, Node, jQuery, Express as well as the Jasmine and Mocha frameworks for automated test coverage. In addition, I understand Object-oriented Programming, MVC framework and REST API's.

To go along with my programming interests, I am also an avid NBA enthusiast who also enjoys travelling to new places, learning electric guitar, keeping a consistent exercise routine, and playing board games with friends.
